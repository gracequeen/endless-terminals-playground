PROBLEM:
Incremental backups were producing corrupt archives that failed during extraction
with "unexpected EOF" or truncated files.

ROOT CAUSE:
Race condition in incremental.sh - the timestamp file was being updated BEFORE
the tar process completed. The script backgrounded tar with '&', immediately
updated the timestamp, then waited for tar to finish.

THE BUG (lines 13-16 of original script):
    find "$SOURCE" -type f -newer "$STATE_DIR/reference" -print0 | \\

    wait $TAR_PID
