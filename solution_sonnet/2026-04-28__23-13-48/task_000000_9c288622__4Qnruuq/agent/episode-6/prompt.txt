EXPLANATION:
  On March 31, 2024, Europe/Berlin switched to DST at 2:00am.
  Clocks jumped forward: 01:59:59 CET → 03:00:00 CEST
  The hour from 2:00-2:59 doesn't exist!

  The bug: localize() on a naive datetime assumes it's already
           in that timezone, so 01:30 UTC became 01:30 CET.

  The fix: First mark as UTC, then convert to Berlin timezone,
           which properly adds the DST offset (+2 hours).
